Lowering the cost of Sanitary items for people in urban settlements.

EsVendo is a social enterprise working with women living in Kenyan slums to provide access to sanitary towels and diapers . We do this using mobile phone technology and innovative dispensing tools

We distribute high quality disposable Diapers that are made of cloth or synthetic disposable materials and supplied by experienced manufacturer.

Our top quality pads are  usually individually wrapped so they are easier and more discreet to carry in a purse or bag. build of quality material.
