EsVendo uses smart vending machines located in safe areas around the slum to distribute affordable

sanitary towels in a profitable way.

 

 

 

 

 

 

The vending machines are located in three villages around Kibera in safe areas.

 

Once the girls access the vending machine, she places a coin and a single pad is dispensed.

Kibera Kiosk 2

Find our vendor opposite kibera community church. Kiosk 25. Remeber to first use your phone to get your coupon code for your discount

Kibera Hospital

Our vending machine can be found the ladies wash rooms at Kibera Hospital. Our machines accept coins only and are eble to return change.

 

Kibera Kiosk 1

Our Pads and diapers can be bought from our local vender shop 228 kibera. remember to first use your phone to get the coupon code that you will show our vendor for a discount.

 

The vending machines that target to service adults in hospitals have an integrated mobile

money system.

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

The adult can place a coin in the vending machine to dispense a single sanitary item.

 

The individual can also dial their number in the vending machine which promotes a mobile

money withdrawal that displays itself on the customer’s mobile gadget.

 

 The customer then authorizes the transaction by inputting their secret pin

 The machine confirms the payment and dispenses the item requested
